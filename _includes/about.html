About Me

Born and raised in Hong Kong, I moved to Seattle after graduating from The University of Hong Kong in last Autumn. While I was trying to deal with a huge life transition, I had a strong motivation to learn. Soon I started coding again.

My very first encounter with coding was a C++ assignment to build a movie ticket selling system in my first year of university. It was a very tough problem since it involved complicated validation logic and many edge cases. For a few days, there was nothing else in my mind except this question. I ended up writing hundreds of lines of codes, aced the assignment and started loving coding. I love it isn't only because of the great sense of satisfaction from finishing the problem, but the adrenaline that keeps overwhelming my brain for days.

For the same reason, I have a strong passion for algorithms. Learning algorithms has taught me many astonishing and smart ways of thinking. All these have been a strong motivation for me to keep learning and coding.

I've sharpened my skills using the following technologies:

  • PostgreSQL
  • Ruby on Rails
  • JavaScript
  • React
  • Redux
  • MongoDB
  • Express.js
  • Node.js

Other than coding, I like studying physics and playing volleyball in my spare time. Thank you for reading through my story. I look forward to connecting with you!
